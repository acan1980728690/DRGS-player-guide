The Thick Boy Overclock turns the weapon's clip of 2 (or 4 with Bigger Mags) into a clip of 1, making 射击速度 become dependent on 换弹速度 rather than the 射击速度 stat.

The math behind Thick Boy:
伤害: Clip size * 伤害
换弹速度: -0.6 + (1/clip size)
