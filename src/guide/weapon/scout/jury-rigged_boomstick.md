The Thick Boy Overclock turns the weapon's clip of 2 (or 4 with Bigger Mags) into a clip of 1, making fire rate become dependent on Reload Speed rather than the Fire Rate stat.

The math behind Thick Boy:
Damage: Clip size * damage
Reload speed: -0.6 + (1/clip size)
